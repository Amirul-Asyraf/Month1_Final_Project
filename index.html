About Me

Hello World.

My name is Amirul Asyraf, but please feel free to refer to me as "Ash" (and yes, like Ash from Pokemon). I'm a budding software engineer with a background in Electronics Engineering and majored in Advanced Computing and Communications (sounds fancy, but really isn't). It was during the final year of my studies that I truly acquired a taste and strong interest for Machine Learning and Artifical Intelligence, with hopes that one day I'll be able to continuously pursue these fields (because who wouldn't want a Terminator apocalypse right?).

I'm currently undergoing a 3-month Software Engineering programme under the Malaysian-based company known as, Invoke, having recently completed my first month of the programme in Front-end Development and will be starting my second month in Back-end Development. I've learnt and gained so much from only one month of Software Engineering and am eager to learn more in the following months and quickly give back using my newly acquired skills.

I hope to be able to one day bring change to the lives of people in Malaysia (and on a larger scale to people all around the world) via a technological route. Our understanding of technology is still very limited and there are still so many people who's lives can be heavily impacted when integrated with tech and I hope to provide better futures for these people.
